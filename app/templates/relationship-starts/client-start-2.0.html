Confirm a relationship

You must enter a unique transaction code, generated by the solicitor or conveyancer acting on your behalf, to confirm your relationship.

If you haven’t been given your code, or have lost it, contact your solicitor or conveyancer.

Each client on the transaction must enter the code on GOV.UK. Once this has been completed your relationship will be confirmed and your solicitor or conveyancer notified.

To use this service you’ll need to:

  • verify your identity on GOV.UK
  • enter the unique code from your solicitor or conveyancer
  • confirm the address of the property of land
  • confirm what you want to do with it
